About Me

I am a senior majoring in Psychology and minoring in Computational Mathematics at Universidad de los Andes.

My research focuses on the network dynamics of complex social phenomena. In particular, I am interested in understanding the conditions under which inequality emerges when individuals interact in social networks. I primarily use in-lab and online experiments, social network analysis, and agent-based modeling.

I was a research fellow and research assistant in the Department of Psychology at Cornell University. I earned a BA in Philosophy cum laude from Universidad de los Andes in 2021.
